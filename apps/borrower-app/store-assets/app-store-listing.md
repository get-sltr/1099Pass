## Full Description

**Turn Your Gig Income Into Loan-Ready Reports**

1099Pass helps self-employed workers, freelancers, and gig economy workers prove their income to lenders. Connect your platforms, verify your earnings, and share standardized reports that lenders trust.

**WHY YOU NEED 1099PASS**

Getting a loan with gig income is hard. Traditional lenders don't understand Uber earnings or Upwork invoices. 1099Pass translates your scattered income into the format lenders need.

**KEY FEATURES**

- Connect Your Platforms
Link your Uber, Lyft, DoorDash, Instacart, Upwork, Fiverr, Etsy, and more. We securely aggregate your earnings from all sources.

- Automatic Income Verification
We verify your income by cross-referencing platform data with your bank deposits. No more chasing down pay stubs.

- Loan Readiness Score
See how lenders view your income with our 0-100 score. Track your progress and get tips to improve.

- One-Click Sharing
Generate secure, expiring links to share your verified income report with lenders. You control who sees your data.

- Connect With Lenders
Browse lenders who specialize in gig workers. Message them directly through the app.

**SECURITY FIRST**

Your financial data is protected with bank-level security:
- 256-bit encryption
- Secure bank connections via Plaid
- You control data sharing
- SOC 2 compliance (in progress)

**WHO IT'S FOR**

- Uber, Lyft, and rideshare drivers
- DoorDash, Instacart, and delivery workers
- Upwork, Fiverr, and freelancers
- Etsy sellers and marketplace entrepreneurs
- 1099 contractors and consultants
- Anyone with non-traditional income
